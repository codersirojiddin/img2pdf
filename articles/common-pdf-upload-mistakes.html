Common PDF Upload Mistakes to Avoid

Check these before submitting your file.

Many users create a PDF successfully but still face upload problems because of simple mistakes.

Common Mistakes

  • Pages are in the wrong order.
  • Some images are blurry or too dark.
  • The file name is unclear.
  • Unnecessary screenshots are included.
  • The PDF was not reviewed before upload.

Better Final Check

  1. Open the PDF after downloading it.
  2. Scroll through every page once.
  3. Confirm that the pages are clear and complete.
  4. Check that the file name is correct.

A short review before submission can prevent last-minute problems.
