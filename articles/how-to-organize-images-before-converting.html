How to Organize Images Before Converting to PDF

A better order makes a better final PDF.

One of the most common problems users face is placing images in the wrong order before conversion.

Why Order Matters

If your pages are mixed up, the final PDF can become confusing, especially for assignments, reports, and submission documents.

Simple Tips

  • Rename image files in order if needed.
  • Remove duplicate or unnecessary images first.
  • Check the first and last pages carefully.
  • Make sure all images are upright and readable.

A small amount of preparation before conversion can make the final PDF much cleaner.
