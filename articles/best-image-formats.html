Best Image Formats for PDF Upload

Choose the right image type before converting.

Before converting images into PDF, it helps to understand which file format works best.

JPG / JPEG

JPG is usually the best choice for photos, phone camera images, and general everyday use. It often gives smaller file sizes.

PNG

PNG is useful when you want sharper screenshots, graphics, or text-heavy images. The file size can be larger, but image quality may look cleaner.

Which One Should You Use?

  • Use JPG for assignment photos and general documents.
  • Use PNG for screenshots and images with detailed text.

Choosing the right format can make your final PDF easier to upload and easier to read.
