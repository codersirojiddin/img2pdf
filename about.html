About Img2PDF

Img2PDF is a simple online tool built to help users convert multiple images into a single PDF file quickly and easily.

This website is especially useful for students who often need to upload assignments, screenshots, handwritten work, or document photos in PDF format.

The goal of Img2PDF is to provide a clean and easy experience without requiring account creation or unnecessary steps.

The website is designed for speed, simplicity, and everyday usefulness.
