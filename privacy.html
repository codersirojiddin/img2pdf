Privacy Policy

At Img2PDF, user privacy is important to us.

This website is designed to help users convert images into PDF files quickly and easily. We do not require users to create an account in order to use the basic tool.

We do not ask for personal information such as passwords, private account data, or unnecessary confidential details to use the image-to-PDF feature.
