Frequently Asked Questions

What does this website do?

Img2PDF converts one or more images into a single PDF file.

Is this tool free?

Yes, Img2PDF is free to use.

Do I need an account?

No, you can use the tool without creating an account.

Which image formats are supported?

JPG, JPEG, and PNG work best.

Who is this tool useful for?

It is useful for students, teachers, office workers, and anyone who needs a quick PDF from images.

Can I combine multiple images into one PDF?

Yes, you can select multiple images and combine them into a single PDF.
